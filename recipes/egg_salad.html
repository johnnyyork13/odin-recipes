Egg Salad

Tasty egg salad can be prepared multiple ways. One of the best ways to prepare my egg salad is by using chicken products exclusively.

The ingredients are as follows:

  • Boiled Eggs
  • Egg Noodles
  • Mayonnaise
  • Shredded Chicken
  • Salt
  • Black Pepper

Instructions:

  1. Boil eggs in a pot.
  2. Boil noodles.
  3. Combine noodles and eggs in a container.
  4. Add 1 cup mayonnaise and 2 cans of shredded chicken to bowl.
  5. Stir everything together and top with salt and pepper to desired taste.
  6. Enjoy!
